About Me

I am Johndave Lausa, a dedicated student currently enrolled at Caraga State University-Main Campus, where I actively engage in the pursuit of knowledge and creative expression. My forte lies in the realm of creating intricate and captivating realism art, a craft to which I bring unwavering passion. Within this expansive field, my skills span various forms of artistic expression, including the nuanced strokes of painting, the delicate artistry of portraits, and the dynamic world of graphic design. I find immense joy and fulfillment in exploring the depths of my creativity, translating emotions and ideas into visually compelling works of art.
